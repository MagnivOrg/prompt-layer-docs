---
title: "Get Table Sheet Score History"
openapi: "GET /api/public/v2/tables/{table_id}/sheets/{sheet_id}/versions/score-history"
description: "Retrieve Table sheet score history."
---

Retrieve score-history points across Table sheet versions.

Use `range` to limit the history window, `resolution` to control sampling, and `max_points` to cap the number of returned points.
